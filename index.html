DOMAIN DEEP DIVES
AGI Race
The United States maintains a meaningful lead in frontier model capabilities and talent concentration, but China is closing the gap rapidly through state-directed investment and data advantages. The race is increasingly about compute access and energy availability. Whoever achieves AGI first will gain decisive advantages across military, economic, and narrative domains.
Strategic Implication: The AGI race is now the central strategic competition of the 21st century. It is influencing alliance formation, energy policy, and military modernization timelines on both sides.
Drone & Autonomous Warfare
Ukraine has demonstrated the transformative power of low-cost FPV drones and Lancet strikes. China currently leads in production scale and swarming technology, while the US maintains advantages in AI autonomy and electronic warfare resistance. This race is accelerating with major implications for a potential Taiwan conflict.
Critical Linkage: Drone attacks on energy infrastructure are currently degrading China's energy resilience, which increases their vulnerability in any future conflict over Taiwan.
